Yes! SolveIt offers a free plan with 5 uses per day and access to 2 tools in English. No credit card required. You can upgrade to a paid plan at any time to unlock unlimited uses, all tools, and multiple languages.

While ChatGPT requires users to know how to write good prompts, SolveIt is designed for non-technical users. You simply select a tool and type naturally — SolveIt handles the complexity. We also support more languages natively and offer a more affordable pricing model.

Absolutely. We take privacy seriously. Your conversations are not stored permanently, never sold to third parties, and are processed securely. We comply with international data protection standards.

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no questions asked. You will continue to have access to your paid features until the end of your billing period. No hidden fees or cancellation penalties.
